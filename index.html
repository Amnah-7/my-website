Frequently Asked Questions

What documents do I need to rent a car?

You need a valid driver's license, national ID card, and proof of address. International customers may need a passport and visa.

Are there any age restrictions for renting a car?

Drivers must be at least 21 years old. Additional fees may apply for drivers under 25.

What is included in the rental price?

The rental price includes the car, basic insurance, and unlimited mileage. Fuel, additional drivers, and GPS are extra.

Can I cancel or modify my booking?

Yes, you can cancel or modify your booking up to 24 hours before pickup for a full refund. Changes may incur fees.

Do you offer one-way rentals?

Yes, one-way rentals are available between major cities in Pakistan with an additional drop-off fee.

What if the car breaks down?

We provide 24/7 roadside assistance. Contact our support team immediately, and we'll arrange a replacement vehicle.
